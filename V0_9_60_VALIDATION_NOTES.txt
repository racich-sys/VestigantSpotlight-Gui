V0_9_60 validation notes

Scope:
- Restore AFF4/APFS and Raw IMG/DD source-type visibility in the GUI as staged image workflows.
- Change processing telemetry throughput units from MiB/MiB/s to decimal MB/MB/s.
- Remove the unused srcType compile warning path by logging staged image source selections before worker start.

Forensic/product rationale:
- AFF4/APFS remains important for macOS forensic images.
- Raw IMG/DD remains important for external media attached to Macs, including exFAT devices containing Spotlight indexes.
- Staged labels preserve investigator clarity without falsely claiming fully implemented image-backed extraction in every path.
- MB is used in the GUI because it is more widely understood by investigators than MiB.

Validation performed in this environment:
- Source inspection and targeted patching against V0_9_59.
- C++ syntax/build validation where available.
- Package integrity validation.

Windows/MSVC validation required:
